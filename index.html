Myco Hacklab is a network of people interested in researching, experimenting or developing projects that combine fungi with disciplines such as art, science or technology. We are gathering and building our own equipment and infrastructure needed for growing mushrooms.

Meet with us!

Myco meetups

A Myco Meetup is a get-together for mushroom enthusiasts and people that are interested in learning more about fungi. Helsieni hosts a Myco Meetup in Helsinki every 2nd Friday of the month at 18:00. Sometimes a meetup is preceded by a relevant workshop with topics like cloning mushrooms, how to make agar or even how to make a glove box. For more details and other upcoming events check the Myco Hacklab Finland facebook page.
